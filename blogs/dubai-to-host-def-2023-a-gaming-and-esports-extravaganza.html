Dubai to Host DEF 2023, a Gaming and Esports Extravaganza!

Dubai is set to host the second edition of the Gaming & Esports Festival (DEF), inviting the global gaming community to five action-packed days of events and activities.

DEF 2023 promises an unforgettable experience with the GameExpo Summit, regional and school tournaments, new game launches, diverse gaming zones, and the Play Beyond influencer tournament.
The GameExpo Summit, powered by PG Connects, provides a platform for industry leaders, developers, and enthusiasts to share knowledge and foster innovation.

Regional and school tournaments offer aspiring gamers the opportunity to showcase their skills and compete for recognition on a global stage.

DEF 2023 introduces exclusive new game launches, offering a sneak peek into cutting-edge advancements in the gaming industry.
The GameExpo features different zones catering to diverse gaming interests, providing a variety of gaming adventures tailored to individual preferences.

The Play Beyond influencer tournament bridges the gap between online and offline gaming communities, adding excitement to DEF 2023.
