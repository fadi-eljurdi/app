General Media Consultancy Services

In recent years, content production in the Middle East has been on the rise. With the demand for quality films and series increasing, the need for a content production consultancy services has become increasingly important.

One popular area of content production in the Middle East is Arabic and Turkish films and series. This genre has been gaining popularity not only in the Middle East but across the globe. With the success of shows like "Magnificent Century," "Diriliş: Ertuğrul," Bride of Ìstanbul" and "Al Hayba," producers and directors have realized the potential of this market.

We assist producers and directors in creating quality content while keeping in mind the cultural and religious sensitivities of the Middle Eastern, North African and Turkish cultures. We help assess whether the content you are considering to put forward for production is suitable for the target audience and meets the necessary regulatory requirements.

We can advise you on the best ways to tell the story, how to keep the audience engaged, and how to ensure the pacing is appropriate. We have an in-depth understanding of the industry and we provide advice on budgeting, marketing, and distribution.

We also help (or if our clients require us to do so) lead in negotiating complex media contracts with actors and production teams (Full development-to-distribution cycle).

Whilst we do not provide media and production legal services, we can help you secure your intellectual property rights, stay protected and that the necessary permissions and approvals are properly obtained for your filming. We are also happy to put you in contact with our trusted multilingual (Arabic, English, French and Turkish), specialised media and media production legal advisors from the region to advise you on any legal matter that you may encounter, trying to set up your business in the MENAT region.

Overall, we help you create quality content in and for the MENAT region and to bring to life captivating and culturally relevant films and series that will appeal to audiences both in the MENAT region and beyond.
